Common Questions

What happens if I exceed my plan limits?

API calls beyond your daily limit are rate-limited (429 response). Upgrade anytime to restore access immediately.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from the billing portal at any time. You retain access until the end of your billing period, then downgrade to Free.

Is there a free trial?

Pro includes a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to get started on Free.

How does payment work?

We use Stripe for secure payment processing. All major cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay accepted. Invoices available for Business customers.
